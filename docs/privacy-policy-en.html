Privacy Policy

Version 1.0
Last Updated: July 9, 2025

1. Basic Policy

Grooming Checker (hereinafter referred to as "this app") recognizes the protection of users' personal information as an important responsibility, and handles personal information appropriately in compliance with the Personal Information Protection Act and other related laws and regulations.

2. Information We Collect

This app collects the following information:

  • Nickname
  • Password (stored encrypted)
  • Security questions and answers
  • Grooming check history
  • Notification settings
  • App usage information

3. Purpose of Information Use

The collected information is used for the following purposes:

  • Account management
  • Recording and displaying grooming check history
  • Sending push notifications
  • App functionality improvements
  • Providing customer support

4. Push Notifications

This app sends reminder notifications when a check has not been performed for one week. These notifications are only sent when users have enabled notification settings. Device notification tokens are used for notification delivery.

5. Third-Party Data Sharing

This app does not provide personal information to third parties without user consent, except as required by law.

6. Camera Function

This app uses the camera for the grooming check function, but captured images are not saved at all. Image data is used only for verification purposes and is immediately deleted after processing.

7. Data Storage Location

  • Registered users: Data is stored in Supabase (cloud database)
  • Guest users: Data is stored in device local storage

8. Service Provision Liability

This app assumes no responsibility for the following:

  • Sudden service interruption or termination
  • Data loss, corruption, or inaccuracy
  • Damage caused by app defects or failures
  • Unauthorized access to data by third parties
  • Notification delivery delays or failures

9. Data Retention Period

Data is retained until the user deletes their account or this service is terminated. Guest user data is deleted when the app is uninstalled.

10. Privacy Policy Changes

This privacy policy may be changed as necessary. In case of important changes, we will notify users within the app and seek their consent.

11. Disclaimer

The developer assumes no responsibility for any damage caused by the use of this app. Users use this app at their own risk.

12. Contact

For inquiries regarding this privacy policy, please use the review function within the app.
